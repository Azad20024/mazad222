Training For Gaganyaan

Astronaut Training Facility established in Bengaluru caters to Classroom training, Physical Fitness training, Training modules cover academic courses, Gaganyaan Flight Procedures and training on Crew Training Simulators. Periodical flying practice and Yoga are also included as part of the training
